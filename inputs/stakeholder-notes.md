# Stakeholder Notes

## Lecturer

- Needs to create assignments for specific courses.
- Needs to set deadlines clearly.
- Needs to view submitted assignments.
- Needs to grade submissions and provide feedback.
- Needs to identify students who have not submitted.

## Student

- Needs to view all assigned tasks.
- Needs to know deadlines and submission status.
- Needs to upload assignment files.
- Needs to see grades and feedback after assessment.
- Needs clear confirmation after submitting work.

## Administrator

- Needs to manage users.
- Needs to manage course data.
- Needs to configure system-level settings.
- Needs to ensure role access is correct.
- Needs reports about assignment activity.

## IT Support

- Needs the system to be reliable and secure.
- Needs logs or basic diagnostic information for troubleshooting.

## Academic Department

- Needs visibility into assignment completion and grading progress.
